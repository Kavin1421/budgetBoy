# BudgetBoy

Lately I noticed something simple but common: most of us do not really know if we are overpaying for mobile recharges.

In a typical setup, we manage multiple SIMs, WiFi bills, and subscriptions. But there is rarely a clear way to understand whether the plans we choose actually match our usage.

So I built **BudgetBoy** to explore this problem.

## Why BudgetBoy Exists

The idea is straightforward:

- Enter plans used by yourself or your family
- Add real usage and spending details
- Compare against available plan options
- Understand if you are overpaying, underutilizing, or already on the right plan

## What I Focused On

- Per-person analysis instead of generic advice
- Real usage vs. plan limits (not assumptions)
- Clear, actionable outputs instead of complex reports
- Savings shown in actual rupee numbers, not just percentages

## What You Get

BudgetBoy gives a clean dashboard view of:

- What you are currently spending
- What you could be spending
- Where the difference comes from
- Which recommendations are worth acting on

One interesting observation: even small plan mismatches add up quickly over time, especially across multiple family members.
